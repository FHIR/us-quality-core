  <a name="search-expectations"><b>Search Expectations</b></a>
  <p>For Condition, US Quality Core includes <code>patient</code>, <code>category</code>, and <code>code</code> search parameters, with required support for the <code>patient+category</code> and <code>patient+code</code> combinations.
